# Decentralized AI Healthcare Demo Video Script

**Max Duration:** 5 minutes

---

## Scene 1: Opening (0:00-0:20)
- [Visual] Project logo, tagline, and healthcare/AI icons.
- [Voice Over] "Welcome to Decentralized AI Healthcare, the privacy-first platform for secure, collaborative medical AI, powered by Bittensor."

## Scene 2: The Problem (0:20-0:40)
- [Visual] Data silos, privacy locks, frustrated researchers.
- [Voice Over] "Medical data is sensitive and siloed. Privacy regulations make AI collaboration difficult, and there are few incentives for sharing."

## Scene 3: The Solution (0:40-1:00)
- [Visual] Federated learning diagram, Bittensor logo, token icons.
- [Voice Over] "Our solution: federated learning on Bittensor. Train AI models without moving data, share anonymously, and earn token rewards."

## Scene 4: How It Works (1:00-2:00)
- [Visual] Animation: local node training, parameter sharing, validation, reward distribution.
- [Voice Over] "Local nodes train models, share parameters via Bittensor subnet. Validators assess quality, miners contribute training, and rewards are distributed in $TAO tokens."

## Scene 5: Demo (2:00-4:00)
- [Visual] Screen recording:
    - Running the API (`uvicorn main:app --reload`)
    - Submitting a diagnosis query via `/diagnose` endpoint (show request/response)
    - (Optional) Show Bittensor node integration or dashboard
- [Voice Over] "Let's see it in action. We start the API, submit a diagnosis query, and receive an AI-powered result. All interactions are secure and auditable."

## Scene 6: Impact & Closing (4:00-5:00)
- [Visual] Impact stats, testimonials, team photo, contact info.
- [Voice Over] "Decentralized AI Healthcare accelerates innovation, rewards contributors, and protects privacy. Join us to shape the future of healthcare AI."

---
**Tips:**
- Use clear, simple visuals and screen recordings.
- Voice over should be calm, clear, and enthusiastic.
- For images, use icons/diagrams from free sources or generate with AI if needed.
